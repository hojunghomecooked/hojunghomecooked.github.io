Homecooked

Tell Me More!

Features

Our platform allows guests to book dinner events at the home dining tables of talented local chefs.

Social

Dinner groups are organized by shared social interests. We recommend events with guest lists whose company we think you'll enjoy.

Local Events

Events take place in your own neighborhood, down the street, at your grandma's house. Our goal: financially empowering local cooks.

Talk

After events, you connect with guests you really enjoyed. If you match, you can attend more events together and build friendships.

Chefs

Our chefs bring talent and culture from a diverse array of backgrounds.
